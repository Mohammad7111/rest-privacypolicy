Privacy Policy

Effective Date: 2025

1. Introduction

This Privacy Policy explains how Meal Connect ("we", "our", or "us") collects, uses, and protects any information that you provide when placing an order with us via WhatsApp Business.

2. What Information We Collect

When you place an order through WhatsApp Business, we may collect the following information:

  • Your name
  • Phone number
  • Delivery address (if applicable)
  • Order details
  • Any special instructions you provide

3. How We Use Your Data

We use the information collected only for the following purposes:

  • To process and deliver your food orders
  • To provide customer service and support
  • To analyze order patterns and customer preferences
  • To improve our services and menu offerings

We do not use your data for marketing without your consent, nor do we sell your data to third parties.

4. Data Storage & Security

We store customer data securely and limit access to authorized personnel only. Conversations and order details are retained within the WhatsApp Business platform, subject to its privacy and security policies.

5. Third-Party Services

We use WhatsApp Business to receive orders. WhatsApp is a third-party platform owned by Meta. Please refer to WhatsApp's Privacy Policy for more details on how your data is handled on their end.

6. Your Rights

You have the right to request access to, correction of, or deletion of your personal information at any time. Please contact us via WhatsApp or email to make such a request.
